# Sprint 12 Plan

## Goal

Repair memory-node contradictions without deleting raw evidence, hiding unresolved uncertainty, or promoting unsafe certainty.

## Doctrine

- Contradiction is not failure.
- Contradiction is a memory-state requiring adjudication.
- Raw episodes stay immutable.
- Semantic interpretations are repairable.
- Unresolved contradiction remains visible.
- No contradicted node may silently support full-premise action.

## Proof Scenarios

- `contradiction_resolved_by_new_evidence`
- `contradiction_scoped_by_context`
- `contradiction_remains_unresolved`

## Acceptance

- New evidence can supersede stale semantic interpretations.
- Context can scope both sides without overwrite.
- Unresolved contradiction remains visible and blocks overconfident action.
- All changes are mutation-auditable.
